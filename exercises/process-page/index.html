Problem

The Merry Adventures of Robin Hood by Howard Pyle has been a popular book/story since it was first published in 1883. It has been published by several brands and various styles of book covers. While the design of most covers are very well nicely done, a lot of them feel outdated and need a bit of a modern touch.

Solution

Because Robin Hood is a well known story, the design of this cover will have to give a old Medieval/Gothic vibe to it, while still keeping it modern.

Research

Upon further research, I have seen most of the covers for this book are either just text/typography with a few elements of design, or they have pictures with just Robin Hood and a background, Robin with his men, or scene reenactment.

Analysis

Based on the research, the majority the covers was just Robin Hood, not so much of the other characters involved. Also, while the covers involving pictures of Robin Hood are nicely done, no one knows exactly what he or the other characters in the story look like. So (when it come to designing the cover) the characters faces will not be added.
